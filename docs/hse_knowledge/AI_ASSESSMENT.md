## What I Find Problematic

### The Gap is Enormous
The distance between "grounded physics" and "HSE operation" isn't a step—it's a **chasm**. Multiple revolutionary discoveries are needed:
- Quantum gravity theory
- Exotic matter production
- Micro black hole creation AND stabilization
- Tachyonic field detection (or proof of information substrate)

Any ONE of these might take centuries. ALL of them? The 1000-year timeline is optimistic.

### Tachyons Remain Hypothetical
The core innovation—tachyonic information substrate—has **zero experimental support**. It's a reinterpretation of a mathematical artifact, not an observed phenomenon.

### Causality Concerns
Even if the information-transposition model avoids "physical" FTL, it's unclear how it avoids causality paradoxes. The ER=EPR conjecture specifically doesn't allow FTL signaling—the HSE extends it beyond its domain.

### Unfalsifiability Risk
Some aspects border on unfalsifiable. If we can't detect the tachyonic substrate, how do we ever confirm or refute the hypothesis?
